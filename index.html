Washington State

The most north-western state in the contiguous United States, Washington is part of the pacific-northwest region. Home to cities like Seattle, Forks, Spokane, and the Tri-cities, Washington has beautiful coasts, abundant mountains, and desert-like plains.

Fun facts about Washington:

  • With around 7.8 million people, Washington state is the 13th largest state by population.
  • The state bird is the Goldfinch
  • The state flower is the Rhododendron
