Olympia: Washington's Capital

The capitol building, seen in the picture above, was completed in 1928. It overlooks Capitol Lake, which connects to Budd Inlet via a small dam. Nearby are the cities of Lacey, and Tumwater. Olympia is located about an hour South-West of Seattle.

Quick facts about Olympia

  • Population: 55,605 as of the 2020 cenus
  • Incorporated in 1859
  • Olympia is located in the southern Puget Sound region of Washington state.
  • The city is classified as urban due to how many buildings the downtown area has.
  • The average household income as of 2021 is $38,443
