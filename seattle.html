Seattle: Washington's Metropolis

The most populous city in Washington, Seattle is home to iconic buildings and structures, like the Sapce Needle seen in the picture above. It is also home to some of the most successful companies in the world like Amazon and Microsoft, making it a significant tech-hub.

Quick facts about Seattle:

  • Population: 737,015 as of the 2020 cenus
  • Incorporated in 1865
  • Seattle is located in the Eastern Puget Sound region of Washington state, between the Puget Sound and Lake Washington.
  • The city is classified as urban due to the metropolis nature of the city.
  • The average household income as of 2021 is $68,836
